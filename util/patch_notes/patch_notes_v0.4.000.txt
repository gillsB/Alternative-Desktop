### Video background Adjustment
- Can now adjust a video background, horizontally, vertically, and zoom in/out, to better fit users style and viewport.
- Video background now has antialiasing and smoother video scaling (less pixelated).

### New settings
- "Video Horizontal Adjustment" Change the horizontal adjustment of the video
- "Video vertical adjustment" Change the vertical adjustment of the video.
- "Video zoom adjustment" Change the zoom of the video.

### Improvements
- Prevents swapping icons and displays a warning if an error was detected in swapping folders. (Prevents accidental corruption of icon paths).

### Bug fixes:
- Fixed a bug which prevented gif icons from being able to swap folders.
- Fixed a problem with logging non english characters.
